JAMES WEBB TELESCOPE



NEW EYES OF HUMANITY



The James Webb Telescope has finally been launched. A giant step for humanity.

It is certain that we will see much farther and much higher quality images in the universe than Hubble. It is not known what discoveries will be made. But in a universe where the nearest and farthest stars are not different from each other, it is very difficult to think that the end of the universe will be seen, extraterrestrial life forms and intelligent creatures will be discovered.

The most important feature is that it looks at the universe in infrared, not optical like Hubble. If we look at the universe with our eyes, of course, we only look, but we cannot see. You know, looking and seeing are very different things. When we look, we cannot see any physical force. We cannot see gravitational waves or black holes directly. So the James Webb telescope will provide us with very valuable information.
