CONSCIOUSNESS



DOES CONSCIOUSNESS EXIST ONLY IN HUMANS ?



Or is it a phenomenon of living beings such as animals and plants? Do non-living things have consciousness?

Even from the very first moments of the big bang, all atoms, particles, dust and gases began to act according to the laws of physics that are still valid for a certain task. The consciousness found in these substances is the same as the consciousness that still continues today. It never evolved. Was it formed by a white hole-like event from another universe whose physical laws are the same as ours? Or was there a miniature universe that was taught its duties when the time came, awaiting its turn, ready to work?

Why do vitamins from food go where they need to be? Why do electrons always spin in the same place? Why is the water flowing down the slope? Because all living and non-living beings, from the smallest particle at the quantum level to the largest cosmological structure in the universal dimension, have a consciousness.

Man has consciousness.
